Name: LouMotion Designs

Description: The site name, "LouMotion Designs," represents a portfolio showcasing Lucy's early projects and goals as she explores UX/UI and web design. "Motion" reflects creativity, growth, and the dynamic nature of design.

Scenarios: What kind of projects has Lucy completed in UX/UI design? What tools and technologies does Lucy use for her designs?
